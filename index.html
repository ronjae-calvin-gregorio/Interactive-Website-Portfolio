CLAWVER: Claw-Lift Remote Controlled Rover

The CLAWVER is a remarkable robotic innovation developed by Grade 12 STEM students. This versatile robot, which seamlessly combines the functionalities of a forklift and a claw, exhibits a unique blend of mechanical ingenuity and technological prowess. Equipped with advanced sensors such as ultrasonic, light, and infrared sensors, the CLAWVER can navigate various terrains and detect obstacles with precision. Its robust construction and efficient performance make it suitable for a wide range of applications, including material handling, environmental cleanup, and educational purposes.
